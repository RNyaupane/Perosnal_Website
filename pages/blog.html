Sindhuli

Sindhuli Gadhi is an historical fort and tourist attraction in central Nepal. Sindhuli Gadhi is famous for the battle between the then Gorkha Army and the British troop headed by Captain Kinloch. It is situated 20 km above the Sindhuli bazaar which is also the capital of the Sindhuli district.
